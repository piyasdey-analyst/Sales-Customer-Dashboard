
## 📊 Project Title
Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard | Power BI

## 🚀 Headline
An end-to-end Power BI project focused on turning sales data into meaningful insights that support better business decisions.

## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I built an interactive Power BI dashboard to explore sales performance and customer behavior in a clear and practical way.The goal was not just to visualize data, but to make it easier to understand what’s really driving revenue and where improvements can be made. From data cleaning to modeling and final visualization, the entire workflow was designed to deliver insights that are both actionable and easy to use.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
* DAX – Creating measures, KPIs, and business logic
* Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
* Data Modeling – Building relationships and optimizing performance

## 📂 Data Source
* Kaggle (public dataset for sales and customer analysis)

## ❗ Business Problem
Many businesses collect large amounts of sales data but struggle to turn it into useful insights.
Without a clear view of performance and customer trends, it becomes difficult to make timely and informed decisions.
This project addresses that gap by bringing everything into one place and presenting it in a way that is easy to explore and understand.

## 🎯 Objective
The goal of this project is to build a centralized dashboard that brings together key sales and profitability metrics in one place. It focuses on making it easier to understand customer behavior and identify the segments that add the most value. The dashboard is designed so that important insights are always easy to access, helping users make quicker, more confident decisions. A strong emphasis is placed on interactivity, allowing users to explore the data based on their needs. At the same time, the design keeps things simple and intuitive, so even non-technical users can navigate it comfortably. In the end, the aim is to turn everyday data into insights that actually support better business decisions.

## 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough
* KPI Summary: A quick snapshot of total sales, profit, and overall performance
* Sales Trends: Tracks how revenue changes over time and highlights patterns
* Customer Insights: Helps identify which customers contribute the most and how they behave
* Regional Analysis: Shows how different locations are performing
* Product Performance: Highlights best-selling and underperforming products
* Filters & Slicers: Allows users to explore the data from different angles

## ✨ Key Features
* Interactive dashboard with drill-down and filtering capabilities
* Use of DAX to create meaningful KPIs and metrics
* Clean and structured data model for better performance
* Automated data preparation using Power Query
* Simple and intuitive design for better user experience

## 📈 Business Impact & Insights

Through this dashboard, it becomes much easier to spot what’s working and what’s not.
It highlights key customer segments, reveals trends in sales, and points out areas that need attention.
Overall, it helps reduce guesswork and supports more confident, data-driven decisions.
